Squid TV

Created by xBloodSquidx

SquidTV is a build for the open-source media center Kodi. This build is NOT officially supported by Kodi. This build is a skin-setup that will give your media center a sleek look. No unofficial add-ons are included in this build, and all content accessible is legal to access.

If you so choose to use third-party add-ons, legal or otherwise, it is your decision. Users must be aware that there may be consequences from either your ISP or your country if you stream or download content illegally.
